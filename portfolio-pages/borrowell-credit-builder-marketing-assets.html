Borrowell Credit Builder marketing assets

Client Background

Borrowell is a customer facing platform that provides free credit scores, credit education, credit building products and financial product recommendations. They were the first company to provide free credit scores in Canada. Their mission is to make financial prosperity possible for everyone. Through their web platform and mobile app they help people feel in control and optimistic about their financial future.

Overview

Borrowell Credit Builder is a financial product that helps members build their payment history, credit history and credit mix. The product launched in May 2022. Previously users had to sign up for a free credit score account with Borrowell, then navigate to and sign up for Credit Builder. This created a lot of friction and made it harder for marketing to achieve success. Once an external sign up for the Credit Builder project was launched, the ask from me was to create ads and a landing page for it.

A/B testing plan iterations.

Challenges

The main challenge was figuring how out to get to the desired state of the web design with updated branding. We decided to take an approach switching out the overall look and feel, but keeping the content and layout generally the same. Then we could iterate further from there, assuming this didn’t decrease the acquisition rate.

Approach

  • Use animation to help communicate more information in less time.

  • Rely more on the accompanying text and use the graphic to capture attention.

  • Simplify the explanation text as much as possible.

Results

This was recently launched, so there aren’t acquisition conclusive results yet.

Accomplishments

  • Launched dedicated landing page quickly

  • Created a test plan to get to ideal design state

  • Developed an A/B test plan to continue learning


Lessons learned

  • Creating a net new landing page with an A/B testing plan was a new type of project for me. It can take a bit longer to figure out newer projects. Establishing a process from this for future projects that are similar should help speed up the process.

  • Strategically think about what might be the best way to communicate something instead of just executing on the item that is asked. Sometimes it’s easier to explain something more effectively with animation instead of static illustration and text.
